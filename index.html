About Us

Welcome to the Bel Canto Ladies Choir – a friendly, vibrant community choir based in the beautiful village of Sonning, Berkshire. We are a group of women who love to sing, laugh, and perform together throughout the year.

Rehearsals

We rehearse every Monday evening at 8:00 PM. Whether you're an experienced singer or just love to sing for fun, you're warmly welcome to join us. No auditions required – just bring your enthusiasm!

Concerts

Bel Canto Ladies Choir holds concerts throughout the year, celebrating a wide variety of music – from classical to contemporary, folk to show tunes. Stay tuned for our upcoming events!
